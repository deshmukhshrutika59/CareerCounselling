What is Aptitude Test?

Aptitude tests are designed to assess your ability to learn and to think critically. While they are not tests of knowledge, they do require you to apply your knowledge in a variety of ways in order to solve often complex problems.

Aptitude tests are increasingly used by organisations to select the best staff for their teams and develop those already employed. They are also used to help young people make decisions about their future career paths.
